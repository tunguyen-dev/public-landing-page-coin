What is SPV and what is the main goal of the project?
Answer: SPV is a decentralized social network that supports the BRC20 token standard, aiming to enhance the BRC20 token ecosystem by creating NFT Ordinal to encode image, video, and text data on the blockchain. SPV integrates DeFi features such as DEX, staking, mining, on-chain smart contracts, and algorithmic stablecoins.
What are the core features and services provided by SPV?
Answer: SPV provides reliable data encryption on the blockchain, data conversion and mapping, data registration and utilization. It also implements security measures such as data authentication and privacy management.
What is the total token supply of SPV?
Answer: The total token supply of SPV is 21,000,000 SPV.
What is the purpose of the SPV token allocation?
Answer: 4.76% is allocated to the core development team, 91% is allocated to the community for mining over 10 years, and 4.3% is allocated for marketing and liquidity.
How can users utilize the SPV token?
Answer: Users can use SPV to pay transaction fees, participate in governance voting, upload images, videos, messages, and engage with project products.
What is the reward mechanism of SPV?
Answer: Users can earn SPV rewards by participating in trading and other activities on the decentralized social network.
Does SPV offer Launchpad services?
Answer: Yes, SPV holders have the opportunity to participate in Launchpad to invest in products like NFT Ordinal.
What security measures does SPV apply to protect user data?
Answer: SPV utilizes data encryption and security techniques to ensure the privacy and security of user data.
In which fields can SPV's features and services be expanded?
